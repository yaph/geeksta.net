The scientific field of Data Science comprises different fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer programming and visualization. A self-mocking design for data scientists with the text pieces "I'm a", "DATA SCIENTIST" in all-caps, "I do statistics" in regular and "on a Mac" in italics in partly outlined fonts. Great for job, conferences and events where data geeks & nerds meet. The statement is based on a tweet by BigDataBorat, which contains the following definition: "Data Science is statistics on a Mac."
