/dev/null Funny Black Text Design for Linux/Unix Geeks/Nerds

Want a custom version of this design? Send me a request.

In some computer operating systems the null device discards all data written to it. This device is called /dev/null on Linux and Unix based systems and is also referred to as bit bucket or black hole in hacker jargon. This design for expert computer geeks & nerds like sysadmins and programmers shows the text "/dev/null". There are different jocular interpretations for "/dev/null" such as "don't bother sending complaints" or "go away". Choose yours!
