FURIOSO Angry Bear Funny Spanish Language Pun Joke

What do you call an angry bear? Furioso. The Spanish word for angry or furious is "furioso" and "oso" means bear, so furioso sounds like an angry bear when mixing English (fury) and Spanish (oso). A funny design showing an angry looking cartoon style bear and the word FURIOSO below it. The furious eyes and "FURI" are highlighted in red. Great for Spanish teachers, students and anyone who loves the Spanish language and can't resist a terrible pun.
