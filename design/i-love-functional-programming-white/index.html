I Love Functional Programming - White & Red Design

Want a custom version of this design? Send me a request.

A design for computer programmers/software developers who embrace functional programming. It consists of the texts "I" and "Functional Programming" in white and the lambda symbol in parenthesis in a red monospace font. The lambda symbol in this design stands for anonymous functions, which are ubiquitous in functional programming languages such as Lisp, Scheme, Clojure, Erlang, OCaml, and Haskell. The parenthesis represent function invocations and grouping of expressions as used in a wide variety of languages.
