Kreuzberg 36 - Berlin Germany Neighborhood Grunge/Vintage Design Black

Want a custom version of this design? Send me a request.

This design shows "Kreuzberg" and a large number 36 in an army/military style font. Until 1993 Germany used a 4 digit post code. Then West-Berlin was 1000 and part of the district Kreuzberg was 36, a neighborhood known for its punk and alternative scenes. A grunge/vintage style design for people who have lived or stayed in Berlin Kreuzberg, a.k.a. X-Berg or KRZ BRG, and hopefully have good memories worth being nostalgic about.
