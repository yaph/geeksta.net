When something goes wrong with a computer or any device turning it off and on again can seemingly fix the issue. A programmer or system administrator confronted with a bug or hardware issue may well ask HAVE YOU TRIED TURNING IT OFF AND ON AGAIN? This design consists of the unicode emoticon known as "Look of Disapproval" and the question "HAVE YOU TRIED TURNING IT OFF AND ON AGAIN?" in an all-caps, black font on different product colors including heather grey, rose quartz and white.
