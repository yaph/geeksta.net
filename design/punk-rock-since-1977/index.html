This design for born punk rockers consists of the texts "Punk Rock" in an all-caps bended font, "since", the year 1977 in a circle of 40 stars, and "Born to Pogo" framed by 2 pogo dancers based on the D.R.I. logo. The design is especially suitable for people who were born in that year and makes for a nice birthday gift. Respect if you became punk in 1977. Whatever that means for you, keep on rocking!
