Punk'n'Roll - Logo of the punknroll.net Web Site

Want a custom version of this design? Send me a request.

This design features the logo of the punknroll.net website. It consists of the text PUNK in all-caps bended upwards and below it the text ROLL in all-caps bended downwards. In the center is the letter n enclosed by apostrophes, that make it look like a hand making the sign of horns, a greeting used by metal and rock music fans all over the world. The font is red with an orange outline and the design is available on different products on black as well as a dark red/brown tone. For some apparel, such as shirts and hoodies, other fabric colors can be chosen.
