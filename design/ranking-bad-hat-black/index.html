Ranking Bad? Black Hat SEO - Parody Design for Online Marketers

Want a custom version of this design? Send me a request.

People who do search engine optimization (SEO) typically distinguish between black hat and white hat SEO, where the former group uses many techniques that are against the search engine terms of service and the latter group does not. In reality most fall somewhere in between but tend to one side. This design is for the black hats. It consists of the text "Ranking Bad?" in black using the chemical elements Radium (Ra - 88) and Barium (Ba - 56) in a green square to start the 2 words and a black hat on top.
