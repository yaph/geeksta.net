Reinickendorf 51 - Berlin Germany Neighborhood Nostalgia White Design

Want a custom version of this design? Send me a request.

This design shows REINICKENDORF and a large number 51 in a college style font. Until 1993 Germany used a 4 digit post code. Then West-Berlin was 1000 and Reinickendorf was 51. This cool design is for people who have lived or stayed in Berlin Reinickendorf and hopefully have good memories worth being nostalgic about.
