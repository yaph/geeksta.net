Privacy Policy for Mobile Data Shortcuts

Overview

Mobile Data Shortcuts ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your device. All data usage statistics and settings remain locally on your device and are never sent to external servers.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:

  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Required to detect and display your active SIM cards
  • Usage Access: Required to calculate mobile data usage statistics from your device

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality. No data accessed through these permissions is collected, stored, or shared.

Data Storage

All information (including your billing cycle settings and data usage statistics) is stored locally on your device using Android's standard storage mechanisms. This data is never transmitted over the internet.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. It is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this location with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact
