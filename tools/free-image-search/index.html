Free Image Search - Search for free images on multiple sites

This is a meta search engine to search for free images on Flickr, Openclipart, Pixabay, Wikimedia Commons and other sites. Once you've performed a search and results are found you can navigate the results with the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard. The left arrow (←) leads to the previous and the right arrow (→) to the next search result page.

The results come from different web sites and the images have different licenses. They are free to use for personal projects and many of them also for commercial purposes. Be sure to check the license of the images you want to use.
